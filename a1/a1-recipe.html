The Super Secret Chili Recipe

Should you consider yourself lucky to be here?

Yes. The answer is an unequivocal yes. You have landed on a document that reveals the recipe to a highly lauded, heavily guarded chili dish, which has been passed down in secret through warzones, across borders and oceans, and survived the sands of time for countless generations.

Your mission, should you choose to accept it, is to preserve the integrity of this recipe and to ensure its survival into the future, intact and revered as it has been so generously provided to us.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. of ground beef
  • 1 onion
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 1 can of black beans
  • 1 can of pinto beans
  • 1 can of kidney beans
  • 1 can of corn
  • 1 jar of salsa
  • 1 chili mix packet/kit
  • cayenne pepper (if not included in chili kit)
  • shredded cheese
  • rice

Instructions

  1. Cut up onion and bell pepper
  2. Brown 1 lb. of ground beef with oinon and bell pepper and drain
  3. To large cooking pot, add beef with pepper and onions, pinto, kidney, and black beans, corn, salsa, chili packet and cayenne pepper if not included in chili packet
  4. Heat until slowly bubbling and then simmer on low heat for 15 minutes
  5. While this is happening cook rice in rice cooker
  6. When both are finished, add rice to plate, top with chili from pot, and garnish with shredded cheese
  7. Enjoy!

Finished Product

As you can see from the finished product, the great lengths that the brave men and women have gone through to bring this to us have not been in vain. It is now your duty to preserve and carry on this magical dish.
